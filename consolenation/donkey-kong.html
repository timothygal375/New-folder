Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is an arcade game released by Nintendo in 1981. It was Mario's first appearance! In this game, he is known as "Jumpman" and his one desire is to save his beloved Pauline from the clutches of Donkey Kong! Nintendo licensed it to Coleco, who released a version of the game for their ColecoVision system before porting it to the Famicom and NES themselves, which was inarguably a superior version. Donkey Kong was the first game in its genre, and pioneered platforming games as a whole.
